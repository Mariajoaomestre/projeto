The logo concept

The logo of the jewelry brand Alma seeks to be the balance between sophistication and modernity, key factors for the success of the entire brand.The logo creation process has always taken into account how essential it would be to transmit the brand's values. It is really necessary for the soul of the project to be in each line of the final logo and to live up to its origins.

Dark green and white

The tones that came to give shape and life to the brand were rigorously selected. Dark Green is not only connected to the typical strength of youth, but also to the harmony with which the jewels complement and completely transform the looks. White with all its symbolism: peace and purity was the color selected for the brand's main.

As a secondary color and used only occasionally, gold was selected. This color is symbolically associated with gold, wealth and magisterial soul. In addition, this color brings charm and builds confidence. It gives power, persuasion and energy. But when should it be used? The golden color is used only to complete the visual identity of the brand. For example in brand publications in order to enrich them and make them more appealing.

The minimalist

The brand has as guidelines that minimalism is the key. The logo is also minimalist discreet as the jewels however it marks the difference. Earrings, rings, necklaces, etc. No matter what the jewel is ... It is delicate and minimalist. The logo is also.

The commercial campaign

To promote the Alma jewelry brand, a commercial campaign was also elaborated. The main objective of the campaign was to promote the brand and its new logo.A promotional video was developed that aimed to arouse the curiosity and interest of possible customers. The video has small frames of footage interspersed with flashes of the logo in order to announce this novelty.
